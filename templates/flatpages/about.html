About Us

Welcome to {{site_name}} Homework Help!

Every day {{site_name}} helps thousands of students with their homework by maintaining a free, ad-supported question and answer platform which is accessible 24/7 by anyone in the world.

Anyone can ask a question, and anyone can answer questions. No registration is required.

History

{{site_name}} Homework Help was created in 1998 as a simple website which hosted basic school notes for a handful of classes such as history, geometry, and language arts. Over the years {{site_name}} evolved into its present form as a Q&A platform on which anyone can post homework questions and receive help in any subject.

The website, "{{site_name}}", was named after the founder's late dog. The word "{{site_name}}" means "good girl" in a native Bolivian language called Aymara.

In 2019, {{site_name}} began launching sister websites in languages other than English. Our most popular languages aside from English are Sesotho, Spanish, and Tagalog.

In 2021, {{site_name}} Homework Help became a property of {{site_name}} LLC in Texas.

Question & Answer Guidelines

When posting questions or answers, please:

  • Double-check your spelling and grammar before posting.
  • Show any work you have completed, and include any relevant details.
  • Post answers which are helpful to other visitors.
  • Do not post any rude comments.

Voting

Please up-vote or down-vote any questions and answers whenever possible. Voting helps us determine which content our visitors find most valuable and which content needs to be removed.

Unacceptable Content

The following types of content are not permitted to be posted on our website:

  • Rude or offensive language
  • Pretending to be someone you are not
  • Advertising of any kind
  • Gibberish or nonsensical text
  • Asking for or sharing personal information
  • Deliberately providing wrong or misleading information
  • Threats of any kind
  • Hacking or attempting to use the website in a way it is not intended
  • External links with little or no context, including:
    • A single link with no detailed explanation of what the link is for
    • Links to search engines
    • References to an external website (e.g. "use Google for more info")
    • Lists of links
  • Duplicate content which is exactly the same as previously-posted content

Visitors who post or attempt to post the above types of content may be banned from posting additional content. {{site_name}} reserves the right to ban any visitor from posting content on our website for any reason whatsoever.
